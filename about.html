About Brand Name Institute

Empowering the next generation of tech professionals

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Brand Name Institute began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry. Our founders, a group of Silicon Valley veterans, recognized the need for practical, hands-on training that prepares students for real-world challenges.

What started as a small coding bootcamp has grown into a premier technology institute with thousands of graduates working at top companies like Google, Amazon, and Facebook.

Our Mission & Values

We believe that anyone can learn to code and build a rewarding career in technology, regardless of their background or previous experience.
